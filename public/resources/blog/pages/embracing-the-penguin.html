Embracing the Penguin: Introducing Beta Linux Support for Beaver Notes!

Daniele Rolli | July 24 2023

Greetings, Note-Takers!
We are thrilled to unveil a remarkable development in the world of note-taking â€“ the beta release of Beaver Notes for Linux! As our user community continues to expand, so does the diversity of operating systems used by our beloved note-takers. It was crucial for us to ensure that Beaver Notes could be enjoyed by Linux enthusiasts too, and thus, we embarked on the exciting journey of bringing our note-taking app to the Linux ecosystem.

The Journey Begins

The decision to add Linux support in beta was inspired by the amazing conversations we had with our passionate community on Mastodon. Your resounding calls for Linux compatibility echoed across our platforms, and we couldn't ignore the enthusiasm and passion behind them. Our commitment to making Beaver Notes accessible to all users fueled our determination to explore the untamed lands of the Linux environment.

Navigating Uncharted Waters: Challenges and Determination

As a team primarily using M1 devices, the process of compiling an app for Linux presented its own set of challenges. We encountered hurdles and complexities that were unfamiliar to us, but we persisted with unwavering determination. It was essential for us to ensure that the beta release met the high standards we set for Beaver Notes, delivering a polished and reliable experience.

A Glimpse into the Beta Release: What to Expect

For the beta release, we have chosen the AppImage format to enable easy installation and usage across x64 and arm64 platforms. We believe this approach will provide a seamless experience for our Linux users, irrespective of their architecture preferences. As part of the beta phase, we encourage you to explore the app's features and functionalities, and we're excited to hear your thoughts.

Setting Sail Together: Future Aspirations

Our journey doesn't end with the beta release. We're eager to extend the Linux support to other packaging methods like Snap and Flatpak, making Beaver Notes accessible to even more Linux distributions. Your involvement and feedback will help shape the direction we take as we aim to offer the best note-taking experience on Linux.

Community Spirit: A Special Thanks
