Installation

To get started with Beaver Notes, visit our official website at beavernotes.com and navigate to the download section. Choose the installer that matches your operating system ‚Äì whether it's macOS, Windows, or a Linux distribution. Once you've selected the appropriate installer, follow the on-screen instructions for a smooth installation process. With just a few clicks, you'll be all set to embark on your note-taking journey with Beaver Notes.

Not sure which installer to pick? No worries! Here's how you can determine your CPU architecture:
  • For Windows: Press Windows Key + R, then type msinfo32 and hit Enter. Look for the System Type under System Information to find whether it's x64 or arm64.
  • For macOSClick the Apple menu in the top-left corner, choose About This Mac, and look for the Processor information. It will specify whether it's an Intel-based (x64) or Apple silicon (arm64) processor.
  • For LinuxOpen a terminal and use the command uname -m. If it returns x86_64, it's x64 architecture. If it returns aarch64, it's arm64 architecture.

Visit Our Official Website:

Head over to our official website at beavernotes.com. You'll find yourself in the hub of all things Beaver Notes, where we're excited to welcome you!

Navigate to the Download Section

On the website, find and click on the "Download" section. Here's where the magic happens - you'll spot the installers tailored to your operating system.

Choose the Right Installer

Select the installer that matches your operating system:

  • For macOS users: Click on the "Download for macOS" button to get the Beaver Notes .dmg installer file. On the download page, you may see a dropdown with options for x64 and arm64, which corresponds to the architecture of your Mac.
  • If you're on Windows: Go ahead and click on the "Download for Windows" button to grab the Beaver Notes installer for Windows. On the download page, you may see a dropdown with options for x64 and arm64, which corresponds to the architecture of your Windows system.
  • For Linux enthusiasts: Look for the "Download for Linux" button, which will open a dropdown with options for x64 and arm64. Choose the one that corresponds to your Linux distribution.

Installing Beaver Notes

  • macOS:
    Once the download is complete, locate the downloaded .dmg file, typically residing in your Downloads folder. Double-click the .dmg file to mount the Beaver Notes disk image. From there, simply drag and drop the Beaver Notes application icon into your Applications folder. You've done it! Beaver Notes is now installed and ready to be your trusty note-taking companion.
  • Windows:
    When the download finishes, you'll find the installer file, usually named "Beaver-Notes-Setup.exe." Double-click on it to initiate the installation process. Our easy-to-follow installation wizard will guide you through the steps to set up Beaver Notes on your Windows computer. Once installed, you can launch Beaver Notes from the Start menu or via the desktop shortcut (if created during installation).
  • Linux:
    The Beaver Notes AppImage makes installation a breeze for Linux users. After the download completes, open your terminal and navigate to the directory containing the downloaded AppImage file. Make the file executable with the command: 
    chmod +x path/to/Beaver-Notes.AppImage

    Replace "path/to/Beaver-Notes.AppImage" with the actual path to the downloaded file. Now, to launch Beaver Notes, simply double-click the AppImage file, or run it from the terminal using:


    ./path/to/Beaver-Notes.AppImage

    There you go - Beaver Notes is now running, ready to assist you in all your note-taking endeavors!
