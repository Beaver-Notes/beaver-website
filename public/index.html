What we stand for: Empowering You through Simplicity, Privacy, and Openness

At Beaver Notes, we are driven by a set of core values that shape our mission and guide our app's development. We believe in empowering you through simplicity, privacy, and openness. These values are at the heart of everything we do

Simplicity
We believe in keeping things simple. Beaver Notes offers an intuitive and clutter-free interface, allowing you to focus on capturing and organizing your thoughts effortlessly.
Privacy
Your data is yours and yours alone. We prioritize your privacy by ensuring that Beaver Notes stores all your notes solely on your Mac or PC. Rest easy knowing that your personal information remains secure and protected.
Openness
We embrace the power of open-source innovation. Beaver Notes is built on open-source principles, fostering transparency, collaboration, and community-driven development. Join us in shaping the future of note-taking together.
User-Centric Design
We place our users at the center of everything we do. Our app is designed with you in mind, offering a seamless and personalized note-taking experience that adapts to your needs.
