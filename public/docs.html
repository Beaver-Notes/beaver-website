About Beaver Notes

Beaver Notes is a versatile and intuitive note-taking application designed to empower users to capture, organize, and manage their ideas effortlessly. Our commitment to privacy and data security ensures that all your notes stay on your device, ensuring your personal information remains in your control.

Purpose of This Documentation

This documentation serves as a central resource to assist both users and developers in making the most out of Beaver Notes. Whether you're a user looking to get started or a developer eager to contribute to the project, you'll find everything you need right here.
